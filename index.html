Research

My primary research interests lie in the field of cryptography, e.g. secure multi-party computation, consensus and code-based cryptography. In 2024 Spring Semester, I participated in a research program focused on optimizing Boolean and arithmetic garbled circuits under the guidance of Professor Tianren Liu. Previously, I explored various topics in programming languages. During 2023 Spring and 2024 Fall, I conducted research on verified programming and bidirectional live programming, respectively, advised by Professor Zhenjiang Hu.
