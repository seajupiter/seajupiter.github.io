I'm currently a junior in Turing class(an elite undergraduate program) at School of Electronic Engineering and Computer Science, Peking University (PKU EECS), majoring in computer science.

I'm mainly interested in cryptography. During the exchange semester in Fall 2024 at Carnegie Mellon University, I've been privileged to work with Prof. Aayush Jain on a project on fine-grained security of lattice-based cryptography. In Spring 2024, I participated in a research program on the optimization of boolean and arithmetic garbled circuits under the instruction of Prof. Tianren Liu.

Before that, I was quite curious about topics on programming languages and engaged myself in research on verified programming and bidirectional live programming in the second semester of the 2022-2023 school year and the first semester of the 2023-2024 school year respectively, advised by Prof. Zhenjiang Hu.
